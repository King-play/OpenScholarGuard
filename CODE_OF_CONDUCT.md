# Code of Conduct

OpenScholarGuard follows the Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct, version 2.1.

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a respectful and harassment-free experience
for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Focus criticism on ideas, evidence, and implementation.
- Protect private research submissions and confidential review material.
- Assume good intent while taking security risks seriously.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private data, confidential submissions, or non-public reviews.
- Sharing exploit details in public issues when private reporting is appropriate.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, or restrict participation when behavior harms
the project or its community.
